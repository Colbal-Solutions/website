Company

Who is Colbal?
We are a small group of retired military veterans with a wealth of knowledge and experience. The majority of tech companies focus on medium to large businesses, with services that are usually cost-prohibitive to smaller companies. We are here to help small, even tiny, businesses leverage the same technology used in big tech to cut costs, increase revenue, and to make life easier. What do we do?
Our mission is to take what we know and apply it to helping small businesses that otherwise would be neglected or priced out by other companies. What we do is whatever we need to in order to meet your technology needs, and if we can't, we recommend another course of action that can.

Where are you located?
We are physically located in Southern New Hampshire and Chandler, Arizona. We can provide face-to-face service to companies within those areas; however, many of our services can be used remotely anywhere in the world!

What services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services, including Web design, cloud migration and process improvement, but one of our more important services is consulting, since problems and solutions are not always clear. We are here to help identify your future technology needs, so contact us and schedule your free 30 minute consultation to see if we are the right fit for your business.
